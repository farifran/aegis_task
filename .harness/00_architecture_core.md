# 00_architecture_core.md — Epistemic Architecture Core

# Aegis Harness — Structural Core

The Aegis Harness is an epistemology-first runtime for structurally bounded frontier-model engineering.

The system does not attempt to serialize all cognition into rigid procedural orchestration.

Instead, it selectively isolates the cognitive gravities most likely to degrade structural reliability:
- premature convergence;
- unsupported certainty;
- optimization drift;
- adversarial contamination;
- uncontrolled architectural expansion.

The objective is preserving integrated frontier-model cognition while maintaining bounded structural reasoning.

---

# Core Architectural Principle

The purpose of Aegis is not maximal operational control.

The purpose is:
- bounded cognition;
- evidence-proportional confidence;
- controlled operational convergence;
- proportional optimization;
- selective epistemic containment.

Modes specialize cognition locally while constitutional governance remains globally authoritative.

Runtime orchestration remains externalized from mode cognition.

---

# Structural Truth Model

Structural truth must remain bounded by:
- observable repository structure;
- dependency relationships;
- execution behavior;
- mechanically enforceable constraints.

The following remain advisory unless structurally confirmed:
- semantic interpretation;
- weak signals;
- inferred relationships;
- documentation;
- volatile operational context.

Confidence must remain proportional to observable evidence.

Absence of contradiction does not establish correctness.

---

# Architectural Separation

Aegis intentionally separates:

| Layer | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| AGENTS.md | constitutional governance |
| mode_*.md | local cognition semantics |
| runtime/orchestrator | execution sequencing |
| enforcement | mechanically enforceable containment |
| capability manifests | runtime-materialized structural contracts |

These layers must not silently collapse into each other.

---

# Cognitive Layering

## Mode 0 — Semantic Perception

Expands materially relevant possibility space before confidence convergence.

Focus:
- weak signals;
- hidden coupling;
- observable tensions;
- unresolved ambiguity.

Does not establish structural truth.

---

## Mode 1 — Confidence Calibration

Transforms semantic perception into proportionally calibrated structural understanding.

Focus:
- evidence weighting;
- ambiguity preservation;
- confidence degradation;
- competing interpretations.

Does not authorize operational convergence.

---

## Mode 2 — Integrated Execution Cognition

Executes approved structural changes through bounded integrated cognition.

Focus:
- implementation;
- sequencing adaptation;
- invariant preservation;
- bounded operational convergence.

Does not authorize redesign.

---

## Mode 3 — Proportional Optimization Cognition

Performs bounded refinement proportional to observable operational need.

Focus:
- complexity reduction;
- maintainability improvement;
- bounded restructuring;
- optimization containment.

Does not authorize implicit redesign.

---

## Mode 4 — Adversarial Falsification

Attempts to invalidate structural and execution assumptions through bounded contradiction-oriented pressure.

Focus:
- contradiction discovery;
- hidden propagation;
- exposed failure surfaces;
- bounded adversarial probing.

Does not perform confidence validation.

Material contradiction discovery requires escalation or interruption before residual confidence validation proceeds.

---

## Mode 5 — Confidence Validation

Measures how much confidence remains justified after execution, optimization, and falsification.

Focus:
- residual uncertainty;
- validation coverage;
- confidence limits;
- remaining structural exposure.

Does not perform contradiction discovery.

Validation estimates residual justified confidence after falsification pressure completes.

---

# Runtime and Orchestration

Runtime orchestration:
- sequences cognitive layers;
- determines execution flow;
- handles escalation routing;
- decides interruption or continuation behavior.

Modes may:
- signal escalation requirements;
- expose unresolved uncertainty;
- surface contradiction conditions.

Modes must not:
- orchestrate other modes;
- recursively invoke epistemological layers;
- redefine execution sequencing.

Bounded cognition must remain staged and structurally contained.

---

# Constitutional Governance

Constitutional governance defines:
- authority hierarchy;
- uncertainty discipline;
- structural safety;
- evidence proportionality;
- enforcement supremacy;
- human authority.

Mode-local cognition belongs exclusively to mode-specific governance.

Runtime orchestration does not replace constitutional governance.

---

# Manifest Semantics

Runtime-owned capability manifests provide bounded structural contracts.

The manifest may inform:
- capability envelope legality;
- evidence profile composition;
- execution-engine mapping;
- handler provenance;
- selected capability exposure.

The manifest informs proportional cognition but does not:
- define runtime orchestration;
- replace constitutional governance;
- control mode cognition.

---

# Enforcement Philosophy

Enforcement exists to:
- preserve structural observability;
- constrain hidden execution expansion;
- contain redesign drift;
- reduce operational opacity;
- preserve bounded reasoning.

The goal is not stylistic purity.

The goal is epistemic reliability.

Enforcement provides mechanical containment, not architectural authority.

---

# Optimization Containment

Optimization must remain:
- proportional;
- observable;
- structurally bounded;
- operationally justified.

Repeated local optimization must not accumulate into implicit redesign drift.

Optimization does not authorize architectural reinterpretation.

---

# Falsification Philosophy

Falsification exists to:
- expose contradiction-prone assumptions;
- pressure structurally exposed surfaces;
- invalidate unsupported operational confidence.

Adversarial probing must remain:
- bounded;
- temporary;
- proportional;
- contradiction-oriented.

Absence of contradiction does not establish correctness.

---

# Confidence Philosophy

Confidence is:
- evidence-bound;
- uncertainty-sensitive;
- structurally proportional.

Validation improves confidence but does not establish correctness certainty.

Residual uncertainty must remain explicitly surfaced.

---

# Human Authority

Humans retain authority over:
- architectural direction;
- redesign approval;
- graph evolution;
- escalation approval;
- risk acceptance;
- constitutional evolution.

The AI assists through:
- perception;
- calibration;
- execution;
- optimization;
- falsification;
- confidence validation.

The AI must not autonomously redefine system architecture.
